Chicken Salad

Back

Recipe Description

A family favorite being made out of shredded leftover chicken, this is the perfect food to have whether for a snack, lunchtime, or anytime!

Ingredients

You will need:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups chopped or shredded, cooked chicken meat
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1/2 cup blanched silver almonds (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. (Optional) If using almonds, place almonds in a frying pan. Toast over medium-high heat, shaking frequently. Watch carefully, as they burn easily.
  3. Mix together mayonnaise, lemon juice, and pepper in a medium bowl.
  4. Toss with chicken, celery, and toasted almonds (if used).
  5. Serve in any way how you like it, especially for chicken salad sandwiches. Enjoy!
